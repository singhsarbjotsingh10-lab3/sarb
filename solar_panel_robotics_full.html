Robots that keep solar arrays efficient — autonomously.

Our solar-panel robotics platform performs automated cleaning, panel-level inspection, and sun-tracking calibration to maximize energy yield with minimal human intervention.

  • Autonomous Cleaning
    Soft brushes + waterless squeegee mode
  • Smart Inspection
    Thermal & visual anomaly detection
  • Sun Tracking
    Auto-tilt calibration for fixed-tilt arrays
  • Fleet Management
    Remote monitoring + OTA updates

How it works — at a glance

Navigation

LiDAR + wheel odometry + visual SLAM for safe traversal between panels and arrays.

Cleaning System

Electrostatic brushheads and optional water-misting for stubborn dust and bird droppings.

Edge Safety

Tilt sensors and bumpers to prevent falls and protect panels.
